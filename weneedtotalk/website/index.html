#WeNeedToTalk about Mental Health.

We’re students at RIT - and we want more mental health resources.

Our mission statement is:

To improve the mental health care on campus, by funding and expanding RIT’s health services to fit the needs of, and reflect the demographics of, the RIT student body

Let's break that down...

We want to fund / expand the mental health resources on campus (Student health and CaPS) until:

  • They can meet the needs of students
  • They reflect on the demographics of the students - specifically underrepresented groups

What are we doing about this?

Right now we’re doing a lot of planning - We’ll have more info published here the evening of the 5th.
